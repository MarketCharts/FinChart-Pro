FinChart Pro - Quick Start Tutorial
=====================================

Getting Started with Your Financial Data Visualizer
---------------------------------------------------

Step 1: Load Your Data
----------------------
- Click "Open File" or use Ctrl+O
- Select your CSV file (supports Yahoo Finance, Investing.com, CoinMarketCap formats)
- For multiple datasets: Load additional files using "Add Dataset" (Up to 5)
- Your data will automatically sync and display in the preview

Step 2: Customize Your Chart
----------------------------
Quick Customization Panel:
- Line Style: Color, width, glow effects
- Markers: Size, color, position
- Background: Choose from professional themes (Crypto, Stock, Gold, etc.)
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9, 4:3, 1:1, or custom ratios

Note: Changes appear instantly in the live preview.

Step 3: Add Professional Touches
--------------------------------
- Chart Title: Enable and customize your title
- Axis Labels: Add meaningful X/Y axis descriptions
- Legend: Useful for multi dataset comparisons
- Events: Highlight important dates (earnings, announcements)
- Grid: Toggle grid lines for better readability

Step 4: Preview & Animate
-------------------------
- Preview: See your chart in real-time as you customize
- Quality Settings: Choose "Fast" for quick previews or "Full" for detailed rendering

Step 5: Export Your Masterpiece
-------------------------------
Click "Export Chart" or use Ctrl+E

- Static Exports as PNG
- Animated Exports as MP4
